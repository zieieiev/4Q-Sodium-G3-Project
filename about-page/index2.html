INSTRUCTIONS:

Depending on the difficulty, players (in this case, you!) need to find the odd-colored square in a grid of squares of the same color to earn a point!

Select one of the three dificulties (if unlocked) to start.

SCORING SYSTEM:

Each round will give a point if you manage to find the odd-colored square.

But there's a catch!

As the difficulty increases, score multipliers will also increase!

1.0× POINTS!

LEVEL-UP

If you manage to finish all the rounds of a certain difficulty, you can manage to move one difficulty up.


LIVES

Unfortunately for you, there will only be one life. If you get one wrong, you have to start over from THAT DIFFICULTY only!
